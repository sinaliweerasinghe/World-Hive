The Life-Changing Magic of Tidying Up

by Marie Kondo

This book is a guide to decluttering and organizing your home and life. Marie Kondo introduces the KonMari Method, a simple and effective way to tidy up your space and transform your life. Learn how to keep only the things that spark joy and let go of the rest.

About the Author

Marie Kondo

Marie Kondo is a Japanese organizing consultant and author. She is best known for her KonMari Method of tidying, which has helped millions of people around the world declutter their homes and lives. Her books have been translated into multiple languages and have sold millions of copies worldwide.

Atomic Habits

by James Clear

"Atomic Habits" is a practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones. James Clear explains how small changes can lead to remarkable results over time. This book is perfect for anyone looking to improve their lifestyle and achieve their goals.

About the Author

James Clear

James Clear (born 1986) is an American writer. He is best known for his book Atomic Habits. Raised in Hamilton, Ohio, Clear received his degree in biomechanics from Denison University in 2008, where he also served as captain of the baseball team. During his time at university he took part at the St. Gallen Symposium twice and won the Global Essay Competition in his second year attending.
